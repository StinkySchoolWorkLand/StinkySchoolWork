Top 3 Numeracy Resources


Khan Academy

Khan Academy is an excellent resource for students to learn math concepts from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus.
The site offers practice exercises and instructional videos to help students understand complex topics.

IXL Math

IXL Math provides comprehensive coverage of math topics across various grade levels.
It offers interactive questions and real-time feedback to help students master different mathematical skills.

Math is Fun

 Math is Fun is a user-friendly website that makes learning math enjoyable. It offers explanations, puzzles, games,
and worksheets that cater to different learning styles and levels.
